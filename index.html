Trusted Energy Transaction Facilitator

AMANI Petrochemical and Gas Limited is an established energy trading and logistics facilitation company with strong relationships across key producing regions. We connect verified end-buyers with reliable refinery mandates in Russia, Kazakhstan, and Azerbaijan.

Our core activity is structuring secure, long-term supply agreements for diesel (D2), gasoil, jet fuel (Jet A1), and selected crude grades — while coordinating logistics through trusted tanker, rail, and pipeline partners.

We operate with transparency, strict documentary discipline, and full compliance — serving private companies, industrial consumers, distributors, and institutional buyers that require consistent quality and reliable delivery terms.

Transaction & Documentation Support
