Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start the process?

Submit your ICPO (Irrevocable Corporate Purchase Order) or LOI together with company profile and banking details. We will respond with available product specifications and procedure within 24–48 hours.

What documents are normally required?

From buyer side: ICPO/LOI, company registration, end-user profile or resale proof, banking comfort letter or BCL (depending on procedure).

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we provide volume-based pricing and long-term contract incentives for strategic partnerships. Contact our sales team for details.

What certifications do you hold?

AMANI maintains ISO 9001, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001, and API certifications. All products include traceable quality documentation.

What payment terms are available?

We offer flexible options including advance payment, net 30, net 60, and letter of credit arrangements tailored to your needs.

How do I become a partner?

Reach out to our Business Development team at info@amanipetrochemicals.ru. We welcome long-term collaborative relationships.
