Our Journey

Founded in 2000, AMANI Petrochemical and Gas LTD emerged from a vision to provide exceptional energy solutions across the Caspian region. Our founders recognized the need for a partner combining technical expertise, operational efficiency, and uncompromising integrity.

Over the past 25 years, we have grown from a regional supplier to a trusted partner for over 50 major refineries. Our growth reflects our unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and customer success.

Okoye Jamel Rowland
