Our Journey

AMANI Petrochemical and Gas Limited was established to create a reliable bridge between hydrocarbon producers in the Caspian/Ural regions and serious international buyers.

We have built a reputation for procedural discipline, documentary accuracy, and consistent execution — enabling both sellers and buyers to conclude long-term supply agreements with confidence.

2006

Incorporated in Lagos with initial representative presence in Moscow

2012

First multi-year diesel supply facilitation agreements completed

2018

Expanded scope to include jet fuel and selected crude grades

Today

Recognized partner in structured international energy transactions

Our Leadership

Okoye Jamel Rowland

Founder & Chief Executive

With more than 25 years of international experience in energy trading and logistics coordination, Okoye Rowland established AMANI to provide a dependable platform for structured hydrocarbon transactions.

His focus remains on procedural integrity, documentary precision, and long-term relationship building between producers and end-users.
